How It Works

The Bespoke Design
Journey

Experience the beautiful, step-by-step process of co-creating your custom jewellery piece with our master artisans.

01

01. Design Consultation

Share your ideas, reference images, or rough sketches with our team. We discuss your preferences regarding style, size, metal weight, gemstones, and budget to align on a creative direction.

02

02. CAD & 3D Visualisation

Our digital design specialists create a detailed 3D computer-aided model (CAD) of your piece. This allows you to view your jewellery from all angles and make refinements before crafting begins.

03

03. Curation of Materials

Choose your gold purity preference (certified 22K or 18K) and select diamonds or specific colored gemstones. We source only conflict-free, ethically sourced, certified precious stones.

04

04. Masterful Crafting

Our experienced karigars (goldsmiths) in Nagercoil handcraft your piece using ancient techniques coupled with modern precision tools. Every curve, polish, and stone setting is carefully executed.

05

05. Hallmark & Delivery

Your finished piece undergoes strict quality inspection and is certified with the government-approved BIS Hallmark for gold purity. You receive your heirloom jewellery boxed beautifully and ready to cherish.
